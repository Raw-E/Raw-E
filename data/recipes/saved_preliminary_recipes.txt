Name: Spicy Idiot-Proof Vegan Lentil Stew
Ingredients:
- 1 cup dried lentils
- 1 onion, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tbsp olive oil
Instructions:
- Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
- Add onions, garlic, and bell pepper. Cook until onions are translucent.
- Add cumin, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
- Add lentils, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil.
- Reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until lentils are tender.
- Taste and adjust seasonings if needed.
- Serve hot and enjoy!

Name: Choop-a-licious Avocado Lime Tart
Ingredients:
- 2 cups raw cashews, soaked for 4 hours and drained
- 1 ripe avocado
- 1/2 cup lime juice
- 1/2 cup agave syrup
- 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 cup dates, pitted
- 1/2 cup almonds
- 1/2 cup shredded coconut
Instructions:
- To make the crust, choop the dates, almonds, and shredded coconut in a food processor until they stick together. Press this mixture firmly into the bottom of an 8-inch tart pan.
- For the filling, blend the soaked cashews, ripe avocado, lime juice, agave syrup, melted coconut oil, vanilla extract, and sea salt in a blender until smooth and creamy.
- Pour the filling over the crust and smooth the top with a spatula.
- Freeze the tart for at least 4 hours or until set.
- Before serving, let the tart sit at room temperature for 10 minutes to soften slightly. Garnish with lime slices or zest if desired.

Ingredients:

- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 apelet (small yellow bell pepper), diced
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 carrot, sliced
- 1 zucchini, sliced
- 1/2 cup red cabbage, shredded

- Salt and pepper to taste

- 1/2 cup cooked quinoa

Name: Broccoli Breakdown Bowl
Ingredients:
- 1 head of broccoli, chopped into florets
- 1 cup of quinoa
- 2 cups of vegetable broth
- 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1 tablespoon of olive oil
- 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
- Salt and pepper to taste
- A handful of pumpkin seeds
- 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon of tahini
Instructions:
- Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
- On a baking sheet, toss the broccoli florets and chickpeas with olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Spread them out in an even layer.
- Roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender and chickpeas are crispy.
- While the broccoli and chickpeas are roasting, rinse the quinoa under cold water. In a medium saucepan, bring the quinoa and vegetable broth to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes, or until all the liquid is absorbed. Remove from the heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes. Then fluff with a fork.
- In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, tahini, and a pinch of salt to make the dressing.
- To assemble the bowls, divide the quinoa among the bowls, top with roasted broccoli and chickpeas, avocado slices, and pumpkin seeds.
- Drizzle the tahini lemon dressing over the bowls before serving.

Name: Uncreaturely Delight Raw Vegan Sushi
Ingredients:
- 1 cup of raw cauliflower florets
- 1 tablespoon of nutritional yeast
- 1/2 teaspoon of salt
- 1 avocado, sliced
- 1 small cucumber, julienned
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 1 red bell pepper, julienned
- 1/4 cup of sprouts (alfalfa or radish)
- Nori seaweed sheets
Instructions:
- In a food processor, pulse the raw cauliflower florets with nutritional yeast and salt until it resembles rice. Be careful not to overprocess.
- Lay a nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat or a piece of parchment paper.
- Spread a thin layer of the cauliflower mixture over the nori, leaving about an inch empty at the far end of the sheet.
- Arrange slices of avocado, cucumber, carrot, red bell pepper, and sprouts on the cauliflower near the edge closest to you.
- Carefully start rolling the nori sheet tightly from the edge closest to you, using the mat or parchment to help. Before completing the roll, wet the empty strip of nori to seal the sushi roll.
- Use a sharp knife to slice the roll into 6-8 pieces. Wet the knife between cuts for cleaner slices.
- Serve immediately or chill for a short time before serving.

Name: Daywork Delight Spring Rolls
Ingredients:
- 2 large carrots, julienned
- 1 cucumber, julienned
- 1 large ripe avocado, thinly sliced
- 1 small bunch of fresh cilantro
- 1 cup of purple cabbage, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup of raw cashews
- 1/4 cup of sesame seeds
- 8 large rice paper wrappers
- For the dipping sauce:
- 1/4 cup of tahini
- 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
- 2 tablespoons of tamari or soy sauce
- Juice of 1 lime
- Water to thin, as needed
Instructions:
- Prepare all your vegetables and set them aside on a large plate for easy assembly.
- Fill a large shallow dish with warm water. Dip one rice paper wrapper in the water for about 15-20 seconds until it's just pliable but not too soft.
- Lay the wrapper on a clean, slightly wet surface. Place a small amount of carrots, cucumber, avocado slices, cilantro, purple cabbage, cashews, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds near the bottom of the wrapper.
- Carefully fold the bottom of the wrapper up over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll tightly, similar to a burrito. Place the roll on a plate and cover with a damp cloth to keep it fresh. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and filling.
- To make the dipping sauce, whisk together tahini, maple syrup, tamari (or soy sauce), and lime juice in a small bowl. Add water as needed to achieve the desired consistency.
- Serve the spring rolls with the dipping sauce on the side.

Name: Rabbleproof Rainbow Veggie Stir-Fry
Ingredients:
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch fresh ginger, grated
- 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 1 cup purple cabbage, shredded
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 1 zucchini, julienned
- 1 cup snap peas
- 2 tbsp soy sauce (or tamari for gluten-free option)
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Sesame seeds for garnish
- Chopped green onions for garnish
Instructions:
- Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
- Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, sautéing for about 1 minute until fragrant.
- Increase the heat to high and add the sliced red, yellow, and green bell peppers, stirring frequently for about 2 minutes.
- Mix in the shredded purple cabbage, julienned carrot, and julienned zucchini, continuing to stir for another 2 minutes.
- Add the snap peas to the pan, cooking for an additional minute.
- Reduce the heat to medium. Pour in the soy sauce, maple syrup, and sesame oil, mixing well to coat all the vegetables. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
- Cook for another 2 minutes, until all vegetables are tender but still crisp.
- Remove from heat. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions before serving.
